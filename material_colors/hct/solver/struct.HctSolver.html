Finds an sRgb color with the given hue, chroma, and L*, if possible.

Returns a hexadecimal representing a sRgb color with its hue, chroma, and L* sufficiently close to hue_degrees, chroma, and lstar, respectively. If it is impossible to satisfy all three constraints, the hue and L* will be sufficiently close, and the chroma will be maximized.
